Training the workforce

This is - what we do :

A well detailed Training schedule is adhered to for applicants to get comfortable with the AI tasks. Trainers begin the training with an open mind,to ensure everyone is involved and understands.

TRAINING PROCESS

  • Step One

    Registration Signing Up to Lish AI Labs Portal,Filling out the application form and submit necessary documents to apply. (ie curriculum vitae,photocopy of national identity card)

  • Step Two

    Assessment Information and documents will be viewed while one undergoes interview'

  • Step Three

    Orientation Learning more about Lish AI labs,what is done their Mission and Vision. Briefing on AI and Machine Learning.

  • Step Four

    Training Get offered a hands on training by well-experienced and trained supervisors. They help create a form`idable task force for the work to be done.

Week 1: ONBOARDING PROCESS IN LISH AI LABS

Day 1

Online signup,one on one interviews, orientation and overview of lish AI labs
1. Online signup of individual details in the LISH AI platform.
2. Filtering and onboarding of successful cloud workers.
3. Introducing cloud workers to LISH AI LABS' mission, vision, and values.
4. Provide an overview of the LISH structure and culture.
5. Orientation of LISH AI procedures, policies, and Lab Rules

Day 2-3

Overview of the Artificial Intelligence field and LISH AI labs as a company
1. Overview of the Business Process Outsourcing(BPO) and AI field.
2. Talk through the company’s competitive prospects and position of LISH AI labs in the industry.
3. Discuss and analyze relevant industry trends and challenges

Day 4-5

Overview of the Training ie services LISH AI labs offers to the clients
1. Introducing cloud workers to the company’s services ie data annotation, categorization, data scraping 3d annotation.
2. Discuss key features and benefits of each service to the cloud workers.
3. Discuss how these services help solve clients' problems and the importance of providing quality work on time.

Week 2: Skill Building and Team Collaboration

Day 6-7

Technical Training
1. Provide technical training to develop cloud workers’ proficiency in the tools and software they will use in their role either as agents or quality analyst.
2. Go through any relevant programming languages or software applications that are commonly used in the AI industry.

Day 8-10

Soft Skills and Customer Service
1. Provide soft skills training such as effective communication, active listening, and problem-solving techniques to the cloud workers
2. Talk about Lish AI customer service best practices and Client expectations. Provide case studies to show the importance of excellent customer service in terms of quality, output/target, and time management.

Week 3: Advanced Training and Job-Specific Skills

Day 11-12

Job-Specific Training
1. Providing job-specific training to cloud workers.
2. Go through all technical projects LISH AI labs receive from several clients.
3. Go over job responsibilities and expectations.
4. provides hands-on training to practice skills and techniques ie dummy tasks/scenarios.

Day 13-15

Process and Quality Control Training
1. Discuss the company's quality control measures and how they impact the business/company.
2. Provide training on the company's processes and procedures to ensure cloud workers understand the expectations and requirements for their role and what it takes to earn some good money and promotion.

Day 16-17

Payments, Compliance, and Ethics.
1. Discuss how cloud workers will receive their payments, the mode of payments, and factors affecting their payment per week.
2. Discuss compliance requirements such as data privacy and security, anti-money laundering, and other regulatory structures.
3. Discuss ethical considerations and expectations for the company and its employees.

Day 18-19

Team Building.
1. Conduct team-building activities to encourage communication and collaboration among cloud workers through group discussion.
2. Provide opportunities for the cloud worker to get to know one another and build relationships.

Day 20-21

Finalization, Account Creation and Feedback.
1. provide a final review of the training program and answer any questions that remain.
2. Provide opportunities for the cloud workers to offer feedback on the onboarding and training process.
3. Discuss ongoing training and development opportunities available to employees. Account creation for practice tasks.

FAQ

Frequenty asked questions

How long does training take?

Training at Lish AI LABS typically takes approximately 3 months. During this period, you'll undergo comprehensive instruction in data labeling methodologies, annotation tools, and quality assurance processes, ensuring you gain the necessary expertise for the job.

Are there any specific qualifications required for training?

No specific qualifications are required; anyone with an interest in data labeling and AI can apply and participate in the training

What skills will I acquire after participating in the training process?

After participating in the training process at Lish AI LABS, you will acquire valuable skills, including data labeling expertise, understanding of annotation guidelines, proficiency in various annotation tools, quality control techniques, and domain-specific knowledge in AI and machine learning applications

Will I get a job immediately after the training?

Upon completion of training an evaluation is conducted following which positions are offered based on evaluation results.

Do you provide any certification upon completion of the training program?

Yes, participants receive a certification of completion, validating their acquired skills in data labeling and annotation.

Are there any mentorship or guidance sessions during the training to address questions and challenges?

Yes, we provide mentorship and regular feedback sessions to support trainees throughout the learning process.
