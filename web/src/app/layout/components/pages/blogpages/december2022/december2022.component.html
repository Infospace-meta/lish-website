PRESIDENT RUTO: OFF WE SET TO CREATE 1 MILLION JOBS UNDER THE DIGITAL ECONOMY IN LESS THAN FIVE YEARS.

by Lucy Wanjiku.

6th December 2022

President William Ruto takes a significant role as the guest of Honor to launch the first Digital job hub at Kabete National Polytechnic. He resolved that partnerships will roll out Labs across the country at the TVETA Institutions in the fastest time possible under the KYEP Program to ensure the program becomes a success. The President also challenged the PS for ICT and Digital Economy Eng. Tanui to oversee the creation of 1Million jobs in less than 5 Years. On his part, Mr. Tanui conquered that the government is in full support of embracing E-Tech solutions to eradicate the high rate of youth Unemployment in Kenya today. Later, the Lish team demonstrated to the President an annotation task which was a reflection of the jobs that would be undertaken in the job hubs immediately the program commences after the Launch. It was sighted that this would play a crucial role in the online jobs’ creation. David Waweru a Lead Trainer at Lish AI Labs presented his 14-month online job experience at Lish. We see him echo about his life being transformed after he created a second income stream funded by the online job’s earnings. He explained that 14 months ago, he worked at a Local supermarket with a monthly salary of 9,000. After embracing the online job opportunity, he is now in a position earning 10,000 a week for sustainable development at his prime age. President William Ruto also observed that the Konza Technopolis provided and installed the 100 well-fitted computers, TVETA provided the Kabete Institution, ACAT would source for the online jobs, and Lish AI Labs bring in the workforce for contracted training and lab management. He conquered that this initiative under the Digital Economy would unlock Kenya’s employment frame, make youth self-reliant citizens, and spur economic growth.
